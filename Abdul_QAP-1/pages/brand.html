Efficient Hybrid Power

The BMW X6 features an advanced gas/hybrid powertrain that utilizes BMW's TwinPower Turbo technology paired with an electric motor. The integrated electric motor boosts efficiency, allowing the X6 to achieve impressive fuel economy without sacrificing performance. With regenerative braking and an energy recovery system, the hybrid system not only delivers up to 389 horsepower but also reduces emissions, ensuring you drive smarter and more sustainably.

High-Performance Engine

The heart of the BMW X6 is its powerful 3.0-liter BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder engine, delivering up to 523 horsepower in the M variant. This high-performance engine is equipped with Valvetronic technology and Double-VANOS, ensuring optimized power output and increased fuel efficiency. With an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission, the X6 provides rapid gear changes for an exhilarating drive, taking you from 0 to 100 km/h in as little as 4.1 seconds.

Sport-Tuned Exhaust

The BMW X6 M60i's sport-tuned exhaust system is engineered for both performance and sound. With dual stainless steel exhaust outlets and integrated acoustic control, the system reduces back pressure while providing a distinctive growl that reflects the true power of BMW engineering. The exhaust system not only maximizes performance but also contributes to efficient emissions management, adhering to Euro 6 standards for environmental compliance.
